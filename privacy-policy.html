Privacy Policy

Updated date: February 19, 2026

The Archivist is a Roblox bot application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

When you authorize Archivist via Roblox OAuth, we may collect the following information provided by Roblox:

  • Your Roblox User ID
  • Your Roblox username and display name
  • Any other information you explicitly grant access to

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely to provide the features and functionality of the Archivist bot. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me on discord.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

6. Contact
